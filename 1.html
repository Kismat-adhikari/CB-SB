Spicy Chicken Curry

Prep Time: 30 mins Cook Time: 45 mins Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tbsp curry powder
  • 200ml coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with curry powder, salt, and pepper
  2. Saut√© onions until golden brown
  3. Add marinated chicken and cook until done
  4. Pour in coconut milk and simmer for 10 minutes
  5. Serve hot with rice
